The scRecentlyViewed snippet will return products that have been viewed by the user in the past 7 days. 

[TOC]

## Standard Usage

````
[[!scRecentlyViewed]]
````

## Properties

### tpl

Default: [scRecently](../Chunks/scRecently)

### rowTpl

Default: [scRecentlyRow](../Chunks/scRecentlyRow)

### includeTVs

Default: `true`

### processTVs

Default: `true`

### limit

Default: 0

### placeholderPrefix

Default: `recent.`

### tvPrefix

Default: `tv.`
